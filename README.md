## ✨ About

Madobe AI Widget is a fun desktop companion designed for:

- 😊 Improving your mood  
- 🎀 Adding **moe aesthetics** to your desktop  
- 🖥 **Moe desktop / ricing desktops**

The widget reacts to your actions and changes its mood.

---

## 🎭 Reactions

| Action | Reaction |
|--------|---------|
| Single click | 😳 Embarrassed |
| Double click | 😠 Angry |
| Fast drag | 😱 Scared |
